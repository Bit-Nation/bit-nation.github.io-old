#BITNATION: Dipcon|00 // Moscow // 2018

Join the Bitnation team to for DIPCON|00 in Moscow to discuss the various aspects of the Pangea Jurisdiction implementation including legal contracts, arbitration and mediation, the global Ambassador Network, and other issues concerning "Crypto Diplomacy". The event will be unconference BIL style, and everyone will have an opportunity to present their work and projects.


RSVP

Bitnation|Speakers

Susanne Tarkowski Tempelhof

Tarkowski Tempelhof is a serial entrepreneur and governance expert and has worked primarily in frontier environments. She started and sold multimillion dollar US department of defence contracting companies in afghanistan and libya. Her aim is to leverage radical technologies and resilient peer-to-peer networks - to create a borderless and voluntary post nation state governance alternative. She founded bitnation in 2014.

James Fennell Tempelhof

Fennell Tempelhof has worked in almost every warzone from 1984 to the present day. He led major humanitarian operations for 14 years globally and was vice president for an international private security company for 6 years in Sub-Saharan Africa. He was a regional conflict adviser for the UK government for 8 years in Nigeria, Afghanistan, Libya and elsewhere and is the recipient of several prestigious awards for saving thousands of lives in Rwanda and Congo. He has an M.Sc. in engineering.

Daniel Franca

Franca has implemented white-box testing software for ERP developers, and a code generator. He developed and maintained an invitation system and social media services for a silicon valley growth startup and was responsible for architecture and development of gemini media software, and management of a team under agile principles and TDD for booking.com. He is brazilian, resident in the netherlands.

Sonja Prstec

Prstec is a commercial lawyer with 3 years experience representing intentional communities in multiple jurisdictions as legal counsel for liberland. She also assisted civil society improve migration and asylum conditions in croatia as preparation for accession to the European union. She provides legal counsel and advice for bitnation and lives in croatia.

Joffy Mills

Mills is a business development, marketing and communications professional with a focus on cutting-edge technology including AI-driven 3D image analysis for robotic surgery and clinical NLP for computer-assisted-coding for SNOMED CT, ICD-10 and e-Prescribing platforms. Mills is a project management oriented problem solver. He is south african, resident in the UK.

Kenneth Lillieholm

Kenneth is an engineer, technologist and diplomat with extensive experience in investor relations, diplomatic relations, legal modalities and ensuring compliance for intentional communities as site manager for liberland and their investor groups and as a diplomat for the danish government. Kenneth supports Bitnation’s legal tech and external relations with legacy states and is resident in Denmark.

Dr. John Mathews

Mathews is an experienced external auditor in the banking sector in london. For 3 years he was a senior associate for PWC, planning, executing and reporting the transfer, transformation and analysis of large financial datasets to support independent audits. He has an civil engineering doctorate from vienna, austria, and is a hacker and chartered accountant ACA, MEng. He is from the UK.

David Orban

Orban is the founder and managing partner of network society ventures and founder of the Singularity University. He is an alert analyst of the global technology landscape and has a deep understanding of the needs of start-ups. Orban has been a long term adviser to Bitnation and through network society labs is leading Citnation’s TSE advisory team.

Alma Sommer

Alma Sommer is creator of Agorist Marketplace, a website connecting the free market. She is also known as the "founding mother" of the Jackalope Freedom Festival, the first TAZ (Temporary Autonomous Zone). After designing cell towers for seven years, Alma quit her job leaving the city "on grid" life and traveling fulltime. Her first son Neo, became Bitcoin's first Undocumented Human and she just welcomed her second, babyTate. She consults for Bitnation on their social networks.

Florian Lenz

Florian Lenz contributes to pangeas backend. His main focus is on building the mashnetwork used by pangea, research in cryptography and software architecture.

Erik Vollstädt

Global Lead Ambassador Born 1993, aspiring entrepreneur and champion of voluntary societies & private property ethics. Proponent of counter-economics and competing market currencies, such as cryptocurrencies. Represented Bitnation as Lead Ambassador since 2015 at the Riga Bitcoin and Cryptocurrencies Meetup, the iBGEk basic income stage discussion in Klagenfurt (Austria), the Cointelegraph Blockchain Conference in Helsinki and the Zündfunk Netzkongress in Munich. Author of the Bitnation blog.
