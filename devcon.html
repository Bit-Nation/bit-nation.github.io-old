BITNATION DEVCON 01 // Amsterdam October 5-8 2017

Join the BITNATION team to discuss the various aspects of the Pangea development. The event will be unconference BIL style, and everyone will get an opportunity to present their work and projects.

It's a free event, anyone who RSVPs can attend. However, we would like you to bring a small contribution, whether a short talk on something interesting you are working on, a botte of wine, or a cool gadget to play with.

The event will be held at Bitnation's Amsterdam hack space. More information on the Agenda coming soon.

Thursday 5th of October: Dinner and drinks

Friday 6th of October: Holon Presentations

Saturday 7th of October: Radical Tech Presentations

Sunday 8th of October: TSE Ambassador and Stakeholder Canal Cruise
